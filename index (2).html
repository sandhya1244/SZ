FAQ

Common questions.

How much does a typical project cost?
+

Scope varies significantly, but most web or mobile projects fall between $12,000–$50,000 USD. AI integrations typically start at $8,000. We provide a detailed fixed-price quote after the discovery phase, so there are no surprises mid-project.

How long does it take to build a product?
+

An MVP typically takes 8–14 weeks from signed contract to launch. More complex products with multiple integrations may take 16–24 weeks. We'll give you a precise timeline during discovery, and we hold ourselves to it.

Do you work with early-stage startups or only established companies?
+

Both. We've built first products for founders with nothing but an idea, and we've extended engineering teams at companies with millions of users. The process adapts; the quality doesn't.

Who owns the code and IP after the project?
+

You do. All code, designs, and assets created for your project are fully yours upon final payment. No licensing fees, no lock-in, no strings attached.

Can you integrate AI into an existing product?
+

Yes — this is one of the most common requests we receive. We audit your existing codebase, identify the best integration points, and add AI features without disrupting what's already working. A discovery call is the best starting point.

What happens after launch?
+

Every project includes a 30-day post-launch support window at no extra cost. After that, we offer retainer agreements for ongoing development, maintenance, and iteration — many of our clients have been with us for multiple years.
